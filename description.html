How Coco Works

Coco is a platform dedicated to animal protection. By sharing the location of animals in distress and capturing photos, users can report sightings and earn rewards. Join us in our mission to make the world safer for animals!

With Coco, you can easily:

  • Share the location of spotted animals.
  • Capture pictures of animals in need of help.
  • Earn reward coins for your contributions!
